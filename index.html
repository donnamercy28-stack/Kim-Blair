The Footwear & Garment Archive

The Engineering: Martini Glass Heels

The Profile: A flared base that mimics the stem of a martini glass. This design provides a wider surface area at the ground, offering superior stability compared to a traditional stiletto while maintaining a razor-sharp aesthetic.

Care: Always check the heel tips (top-pieces) for wear. Replace them before the metal nail is exposed.

Closet Maintenance & Merch Logic

The Denim Protocol

To Wash or Freeze? We follow the 6-month rule. For raw denim, store in a sealed bag in the freezer for 48 hours to kill bacteria without fading the indigo. Only machine wash (inside out, cold) when structurally necessary.

Artist Merch Strategy

Focus on **Heavyweight Cotton (400GSM+)** and oversized fits. We categorize merch as "Elevated Loungewear," pairing band tees with blazers for the "High-Low" aesthetic.
